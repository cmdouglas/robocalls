Frequently Asked Questions

Is this real? Will my Member of Congress listen?

Yes and yes. This is a completely serious service for anyone who wants to contact their elected officials. Their staff will pick up the phone and listen to your message, which will include your name and the fact that you are a constituent.

What am I asking Congress to do?

Remove Donald Trump from office through impeachment. He is separating families and abusing children, discriminating against our people for their religion, encouraging white supremacists, obstructing justice and working with a foreign dictator to undermine our democracy. Impeachment will protect us from this president and make it possible to build a country that finally works for all of us.

What does the recording say?

“Hello [Representative’s Name]. Your constituent [Your name] from zip code [your zip code] thinks the following recording justifies the impeachment of Donald Trump: [recording plays]. Their number is [your number].” That’s all.

Why shouldn’t I just call Congress myself?

You can! There is nothing wrong with a regular call, but Trump’s confession of sexual assault speaks for itself, and we thought this was more efficient.
